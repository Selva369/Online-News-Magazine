A.R. Rahman launches trailer of Pele: Birth of a Legend.

Oscar-winning composer A.R. Rahman on Monday launched the trailer of Pele Birth of a Legend, which is an upcoming American biographical film about the Brazilian football legend Pele.

So its a lesson for every aspiring father who never made it, the kids will make it. Be firm and believe in almighty. Encourage your kids and give them all your blessings, he added.

With music by Rahman, the film is directed and written by Jeff Zimbalist and Michael Zimbalist.
